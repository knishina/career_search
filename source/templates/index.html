Your career search simplified.

Job listing sites are great tools for matching employees with jobs posted by employers. These sites are wildly successful. Unfortunately, many job-seekers become overwhelmed by the sheer number of listings that are duplicated across sites or fail to match their qualifications.

This application simplifies the job hunt by gathering postings from various job sites. In addition to searching for the desired job title, key terms and travel time from home are evaluated for best match. The top ten results are displayed, streamlining the applicant's search.
